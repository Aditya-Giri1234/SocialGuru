## 🎯 **What is SocialGuru?**  
**SocialGuru** redefines how you connect online. Share your world with posts and stories, engage with your friends, and manage your notifications—all in one place. With robust features like real-time chatting and activity logging, it’s not just an app; it’s your **social partner** in the digital era.  

---

## 🚀 **Features at a Glance**

### 📝 **User-Friendly Interface**
- Intuitive and sleek design optimized for an enhanced user experience.

### 📱 **Posts & Stories**
- **Create Posts**: Share moments with text, images, or videos.  
- **Engagement**: Like, comment, and share posts with ease.  
- **Share Stories**: Add images or videos to your story and let them speak for themselves.  

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **Build Your Social Circle**
- **Friends Management**: Send, receive, and manage friend requests.  
- **Followers**: Follow and unfollow users to curate your feed.  

### 💬 **Real-Time Chat**
- One-on-one chats and group conversations.  
- Share text, media, and more with your friends instantly.  

### 🔔 **Stay Updated**
- Notifications for likes, comments, friend requests, and messages.  
- Reply directly to chat notifications without opening the app.  

### 📂 **Activity Log**
- A detailed view of your posts, liked posts, and commented posts.  

### 🎨 **Media Editing**
- **Crop Images**: Perfect your shots before sharing.  
- **Trim Videos**: Highlight only the best parts.  

### 🔒 **Security & Account Management**
- Password recovery and account deletion made simple and secure.

## 📃 **Technical Details**
- **Platform**: Android  
- **Minimum API Level**: 26 (Android 8.0 Oreo)  
- **Architecture**: MVVM with Clean Code principles.
